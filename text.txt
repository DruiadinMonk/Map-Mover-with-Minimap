Map Mover is a map that moves, but the character (object) does not.


Character
	Circle in middle width/2 and height/2

Map
	200% size of window displayed.

	Small squares on floor to resemble movement.
	
1st Edition
	Only Character and background.

3nd edition
	Character, background, AND a minimap.
	Main view: 	Background moves, showing only character's field of vision.
	2nd view: 	Character moves, showing full map.
